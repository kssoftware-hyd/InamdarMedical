About Us

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner in Bhokar

Who We Are

Care Hospital & Inamdar Medical Stores has been serving the Bhokar community with dedication, compassion, and excellence in healthcare services.

Located at Bhokar - Kinwat Road, beside Gramin Bank, we are a trusted name in both hospital care and pharmaceutical services. Our commitment to providing genuine medicines and quality healthcare has earned us a stellar 5.04 rating on Google Reviews.

We understand that health is wealth, and our mission is to ensure that every person in Bhokar and surrounding areas has access to quality medical care and authentic medicines at fair prices.
